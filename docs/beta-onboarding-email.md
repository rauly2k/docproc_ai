## Quick Start Guide

**Upload Your First Invoice:**
1. Click "Upload Document" in sidebar
2. Select "Invoice" as document type
3. Choose a PDF or image file (max 50 MB)
4. Click "Upload and Process"
5. Wait 15-20 seconds for AI extraction
6. Review extracted data (supplier, amount, date, line items)
7. Edit any incorrect fields
8. Click "Approve" to finalize

**Try Chat with PDF:**
1. Upload a contract or agreement
2. Click "Chat" tab
3. Ask: "What are the main terms of this contract?"
4. Get instant answer with page citations
5. Ask follow-up questions
